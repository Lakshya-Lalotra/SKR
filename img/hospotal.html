CARE ADVICES

Don’t touch your face, eyes, nose, mouth or any open cuts or wounds after touching anything in the hospital room, such as bed rails, tables, doorknobs, TV remotes or the phone. Sanitize your cell phone before using it in the hospital. Wash your hands with soap or use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer before holding the hand of a patient or loved one.
